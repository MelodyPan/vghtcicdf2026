Smart Medical Highlights

1. Tube Preparation System & Fully Automated Laboratory

Location: 2F Blood Drawing Area (Rear Outpatient Building) / 3F Department of Laboratory Medicine

This site features one of Taiwan's largest and most highly integrated smart laboratories. Equipped with the latest CARS (Computer-Assisted Robotic System), it uses fully electric, touchless specimen transportation to achieve low-noise and low-carbon safety standards. The system can process more than 10,000 specimens per day and reduces infectious medical waste by approximately 50%. With real-time data from the Smart Monitoring Center, clinicians can obtain reports faster and make timelier treatment decisions.

2. Smart Ward - Ward 34

Location: 4F, Emergency Building

Through a structured training framework and multidisciplinary teamwork, the Smart Ward delivers personalized nursing care. It integrates clinical nursing data, smart whiteboards, bedside interactive systems, and mobile nursing carts to support efficient real-time collaboration across teams. This model combines advanced technology with compassionate, patient-centered care to improve both safety and service quality.

3. Telehealth Center

Location: 2F, Emergency Building

The center is one of Taiwan's largest telehealth hubs, integrating remote nursing resources, real-time teleconsultation support, post-discharge home follow-up, and smart case management. It helps improve continuity and quality of care between urban and rural regions, while also promoting international healthcare collaboration and technology exchange.

4. Virtual Reality (VR) Clinical Activities

Location: VR Rooms, 2F & 4F, Education Building

As a leading teaching hospital, TCVGH provides high-specification OSCE examination areas, a simulation medicine center, and advanced VR training tools. These immersive modules support clinical doctors and healthcare staff in practicing microsurgery techniques, emergency response workflows, and complex clinical scenarios, accelerating practical skill development.
