Campus Tour

1. Telehealth Center

Venue：2F, Emergency Building

The Telemedicine Center is dedicated to advancing smart healthcare through the integration of digital technologies, 5G communication, and real-time medical data sharing. Operating 24/7, the center provides remote consultations, specialist support, and coordinated care across healthcare facilities, improving accessibility and efficiency for patients and medical professionals alike.

By connecting ambulances, community hospitals, and medical centers through a secure telemedicine platform, healthcare teams can exchange patient information, diagnostic images, and vital signs in real time. This capability is particularly valuable in emergency situations such as acute stroke, where rapid assessment and treatment can significantly improve patient outcomes.

The center also supports hospital operations through digital dashboards for bed management, resource allocation, and clinical workflow monitoring. Through continuous innovation and collaboration, the Telemedicine Center promotes healthcare accessibility, enhances clinical decision-making, and contributes to the development of a patient-centered smart healthcare ecosystem.

2. Tube Preparation System & Smart Automated Laboratory

Venue：Blood Drawing Area, 2F, Rear Outpatient Building; Laboratory Section, 3F, Rear Outpatient Building

Taichung Veterans General Hospital proudly presents Taiwan's largest and most integrated Fully Automated Smart Laboratory. Equipped with the world's latest Computer-Aided Robotic System (CARS), a fully electric and contact-free specimen transportation system, the laboratory provides a low-noise, zero-emission, and highly safe operating environment while supporting sustainable healthcare development.

The smart laboratory seamlessly integrates clinical chemistry, immunology, hematology, and coagulation testing into a unified automated workflow. With the capacity to process more than 10,000 specimens daily, it delivers exceptional efficiency, accuracy, and reliability. Automated specimen handling and tube consolidation reduce infectious waste by up to 50%, supporting the hospital's low-carbon and environmental sustainability initiatives.

Through real-time monitoring and data integration at the Smart Monitoring Center, clinicians can access critical laboratory information more rapidly, enabling timely clinical decision-making and enhanced infection control. This innovative facility sets a new benchmark for smart, safe, efficient, and sustainable laboratory services.

3. Smart Ward - Ward 34

Venue：4F, Emergency Building

Taichung Veterans General Hospital delivers holistic nursing care through a structured training system and multidisciplinary teams. Digital tools like bedside systems, smart whiteboards, and mobile nursing carts support efficient, real-time care. Specialized nurses serve in ICUs and ORs, while ongoing training and quality committees ensure professional growth. Smart wards combine compassionate care with advanced technology to enhance patient safety and service quality.

4. Virtual Reality (VR) Clinical Activities

Venue：VR Room , 2F & 4F, Education Building

We are a leading teaching hospital in Taiwan, committed to high-quality medical education. Our training facilities include OSCE halls, simulation centers, and VR-based learning tools. Focusing on patient-centered and evidence-based teaching, we provide continuous training for physicians and healthcare staff. Recognized nationally and internationally, we serve as a key training and teacher education hub.
